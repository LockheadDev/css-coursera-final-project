Accesibility technology

In today's digital landscape, ensuring accessibility of digital media is paramount. Accessible technology integrates tools such as screen readers, alternative text, keyboard navigation, and multimedia captions, enabling individuals with disabilities to access and engage with online content effectively. These tools not only enhance usability but also promote inclusivity, ensuring everyone, regardless of ability, can benefit from and participate in the digital experience.
